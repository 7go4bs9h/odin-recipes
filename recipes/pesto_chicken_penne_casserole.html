Pesto Chicken Penne Casserole

Creamy, flavorful and so easy to fix. This meal-in-one will wow family and friends. Makes a lot, so freeze some or halve the recipe.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) box penne pasta
  • 6 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 4 cups shredded Italian cheese blend
  • 3 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 (15 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) jar pesto sauce
  • 1 ½ cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
  3. Meanwhile, combine the chicken in a bowl with the Italian cheese blend, spinach, tomatoes, alfredo sauce, pesto sauce, and milk.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and golden brown on top, 40 to 45 minutes.
