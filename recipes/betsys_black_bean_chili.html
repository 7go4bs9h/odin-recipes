Betsy's Black Bean Chili

Great quick recipe for a rainy night. Serve with low-fat sour cream and shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese for topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground round
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans no-salt-added black beans
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chili seasoning mix

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
  3. Drain one can of black beans pour into a large pot over medium heat; add second can black beans, tomato sauce, salsa, and chili seasoning mix.
  4. Stir cooked ground round into the beans mixture, bring to a simmer, and cook until the flavors blend, 20 to 25 minutes.
